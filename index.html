NeuroNest

Welcome to NeuroNest, your go-to resource for exploring the exciting intersection of data science and brain science. Our platform offers accessible and engaging content for enthusiasts, students, and professionals interested in neuroimaging and coding. We provide tutorials, coding snippets, project ideas, and insightful articles on cutting-edge technologies and techniques. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced researcher, NeuroNest is here to support your journey and foster a collaborative, fun, and continuous learning community. Welcome to NeuroNest, where curiosity meets innovation.

MRI

Learn about Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) and its applications in studying brain anatomy and function.

CT

Explore Computed Tomography (CT) and its use in creating detailed images of brain structures using X-rays.

PET

Understand Positron Emission Tomography (PET) for visualizing metabolic and blood flow processes in the brain.

EEG/MEG

Discover how Electroencephalography (EEG) and Magnetoencephalography (MEG) measure electrical and magnetic fields to study brain activity.

Spectroscopy

Learn about Near-Infrared Spectroscopy (NIRS) and Magnetic Imaging Spectroscopy (MRS) and its applications in monitoring brain oxygenation and hemodynamics.

Ultrasound

Understand how Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of brain structures, particularly in neonatal imaging.

MatLab

Explore coding tutorials and examples using MATLAB for neuroimaging data.

Data Preprocessing

Gain insights into techniques for cleaning and preparing neuroimaging data for analysis.

Python

Explore coding tutorials and examples using Python for neuroimaging data analysis and visualization.

GitHub/Docker

Learn about version control and containerization using GitHub and Docker for collaborative neuroimaging projects.

Bash/Command Line

Discover the power of Bash scripting for automating workflows and managing data in neuroimaging research.

Open Science & Data Sharing

Understand the principles and practices of open science and data sharing in neuroimaging research.
